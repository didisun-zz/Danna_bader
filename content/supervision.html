Supervision and reflection on practical experiences

When I refer to reflection on practical experiences, I am addressing people, groups of people at work, teams, or special interest groups who want to reflect on their situation at work or in a volunteer position and/or want to change specific conditions or work methods. Reflection on practical experiences can help:

  • in expanding perspectives
  • in managing conflicts
  • in analyzing situations
  • in improving communication
  • in discussing conflicts and providing guidance
  • in improving cooperation and cooperation within the context of a team
  • in adapting structures to specific needs
